# Bhavin Kamani

**Senior full-stack engineer.** I build software around operational decisions — systems that ingest context, apply explicit rules, call bounded tools, and produce an auditable result.

My current work sits at the intersection of Cloudflare and applied AI: Workers, Durable Objects, D1, R2, Queues, and Workflows wired into retrieval, browser automation, and typed contracts. The recurring questions are simple but load-bearing — who owns the state, which transitions are valid, what the system is allowed to do, and what evidence survives when something goes wrong.

By day I lead platform engineering on a consumer product serving ~100,000 daily users. The rest of the time I consult and ship my own products.

→ **[bhavinkamani.com](https://bhavinkamani.com)** — an interactive, agentic-CLI résumé you can actually talk to.

## What I build

- **Agentic workflows** that automate the decision, not just the click — bounded tools, explicit state, and evidence you can audit after the fact.
- **Real-time systems** coordinated by Durable Objects — matchmaking, scheduling, and stateful edge coordination.
- **Retrieval + applied AI** — embeddings, vector search, and grounded answers over sources a team actually owns.
